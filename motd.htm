Hello! Welcome to The Trans Fortress!
We're a small trans-focused queer community of TF2 players.
We accept all walks of life; queer or straight, human or critter!

Rules

Must be 16 years old or older.
No bigotry or slurs; this is a place for everyone!
No NSFW or sexually explicit behavior/content.
Don't constantly annoy everyone or be rude.
Please follow the rules and report any rule-breakers you see!

Info

Say "!rtv"/"rtv" to vote for an early map change.
Say "!scramble"/"scramble" to start a vote for a team scramble.
Say "!nominate"/"nominate" to pick a map to add to the next map vote. You can also say "!nominate [map name]" to skip the menu.
